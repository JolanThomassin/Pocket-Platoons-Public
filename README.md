# Pocket Platoons - Roguelike Army Builder

## Installation

### For Downloadable Build

Donwloard and unzip the `BaseBuild.zip` folder, then click on `PocketPlatoons.exe` to start the game.

### For Online Play 

Open the [itch.io](https://jolanam.itch.io/pocket-platoons) page and click Play.

Your browser must support WebGL.

## About the Game

In this roguelike strategy game, your goal is to build the strongest army and defeat waves of enemies. Each run is unique: recruit units, collect powerful cards, and plan your strategy carefully to survive all levels.

## How to Play

### Levels & Rounds

The game has 2 levels, each split into 3 rounds.

Defeat all enemy waves in each round to progress.

### Build Your Army

Recruit units with different rarities and abilities.

Strengthen them with buffs before each battle via the Shop.

### Cards

Tarot Cards: grant permanent boosts for your run.

Buff Cards: provide temporary effects to help you during battles.
